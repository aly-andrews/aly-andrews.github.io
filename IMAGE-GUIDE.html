Quick Image Upload Guide

Where to Upload Images

Logo

  1. Site Logo: assets/images/alyandrews2.png
    • Recommended: 200-300px wide
    • Transparent background preferred
    • PNG format recommended

Homepage Images

  1. Profile Photo: assets/images/about/profile.jpg
    • Square image (1:1 ratio)
    • Recommended: 600x600px or larger
  2. Portfolio Images:
    • assets/images/portfolio/shopify-docs.jpg - Getting started on Shopify (homepage featured)
    • assets/images/portfolio/video.jpg - Video production project
  3. YouTube Plaque (near video section):
    • assets/images/landing/youtube-plaque.jpg - Photo of your YouTube Silver Plaque
    • Recommended: square or portrait, max-width ~320px on site

About Page – Photography Grid

Upload to assets/images/photography/:

  • photo-1.jpg through photo-6.jpg - Your photography work (portraits, sessions, etc.)
  • Grid displays 3 columns on desktop, 2 on tablet, 1 on mobile
  • Square or near-square images work best (they display in 1:1 aspect ratio)

Work Page Images

Upload to assets/images/portfolio/:

Getting started on Shopify Section:

  • Placeholder for teach a class video and supporting content (project plan, learner profiles, storyboard, resources). Add images when ready.

Video Section:

  • video-main.jpg - Large featured image
  • video-pos.jpg - POS series
  • video-international.jpg - International expansion
  • video-migration.jpg - Content migration

Image Tips

  • Format: JPG or PNG
  • Size: Keep under 500KB when possible
  • Dimensions:
    • Large images: 1200-1600px wide
    • Medium images: 800-1000px wide
  • Aspect Ratios: 4:3 or 16:10 work well

How It Works

Once you upload images to these locations, they’ll automatically appear on the site. The placeholders will disappear and your images will show instead.

No code changes needed - just upload and push to GitHub!
