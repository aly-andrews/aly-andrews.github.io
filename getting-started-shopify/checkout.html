Configure checkout

Account and field requirements.

Checkout is the final step before purchase. Mobile quality affects completed orders and strict requirements increase abandonment. Keep it simple. Checkout settings control account requirements, information fields and order processing. Optional or guest checkout usually performs better than requiring accounts. Require only what you need for fulfillment.

Checkout settings

The checkout settings page is where you control how customers complete a purchase. You will find options for customer accounts, the information fields customers must fill out and how orders are processed after payment. Use the steps below to open and review the main areas.

Steps

  1. In Shopify admin, go to Settings > Checkout.
  2. Review sections for customer accounts, customer information and order processing.

Customer accounts

Account settings affect conversion. In most cases, optional or guest checkout performs better than requiring customers to create an account before purchase. Choose optional if you want to give customers the choice, required if your business needs accounts, or disabled to remove account creation entirely.

Steps

  1. Go to Settings > Checkout.
  2. In Customer accounts, choose optional, required or disabled.
  3. Click Save.

Checkout fields

Require only the information you need for fulfillment and payment. Extra required fields usually reduce completion rates and can increase cart abandonment. Enable only the fields that are necessary to ship the order and process payment.

Steps

  1. Go to Settings > Checkout.
  2. In Customer information, choose which fields are required or optional.
  3. Enable only fields needed for fulfillment and click Save.
