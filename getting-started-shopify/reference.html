Troubleshooting guide

Area Issue Quick fix
Store settings Legal pages still show old details Update policy pages in Online Store > Pages and save changes.
Payments No payment option at checkout In Settings > Payments, confirm at least one provider is active.
Shipping No shipping options at checkout Set at least one shipping zone and one shipping rate.
Checkout Checkout feels too long Reduce required fields in Settings > Checkout.

Glossary

  • Shipping zone: A geographic area you ship to.
  • Shipping rate: The amount customers pay for shipping.
  • Collection: A group of products displayed together.
  • Variant: A version of a product with options such as size or color.
  • Payment provider: A service that processes customer payments.
