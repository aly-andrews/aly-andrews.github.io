Configure shipping

Zones, rates and profiles.

Checkout cannot complete without shipping zones and rates. Shipping logic affects profitability and incorrect setup can block orders in some regions. Zones define where you ship. Rates define what customers pay. Profiles apply different rules to different product groups. Weight based rates are most reliable when products vary in weight. Most merchants start with the default profile.

On this page

  • Default profile
  • Domestic zone
  • Weight based rates

Default profile

Steps

  1. In Shopify admin, go to Settings > Shipping and delivery.
  2. Open Shipping profiles and select Default shipping profile to inspect zones and rates.

Domestic zone

Before configuring rates, estimate carrier costs, packaging and average order weight so pricing is realistic.

Steps

  1. Go to Settings > Shipping and delivery.
  2. In Shipping zones, click Add shipping zone, enter zone name and select country or region.
  3. Click Done.

Weight based rates

Steps

  1. Open the domestic shipping zone.
  2. In Shipping rates, click Add rate.
  3. Select Weight based, enter weight ranges and prices, click Save.

Accurate product weight is required for correct shipping charges.
