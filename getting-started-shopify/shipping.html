Set up your shipping

Create shipping zones and rates so shipping calculations work correctly at checkout.

Shipping setup determines where you ship and how much customers pay. Start with one domestic zone, add weight based rates and test your checkout flow with sample orders.

Understanding shipping profiles

Most new merchants can start with the default shipping profile. Add custom profiles later if different product groups need different shipping rates.

Creating a domestic shipping zone

Creating your domestic shipping zone

Follow these steps to create your domestic shipping zone:

Desktop

  1. In your Shopify admin, go to Settings.
  2. Click Shipping and delivery.
  3. In Shipping zones, click Add shipping zone if needed.
  4. Enter a zone name and select your country or region.
  5. Click Done.

Adding weight based shipping rates

Adding weight based rates

Follow these steps to add weight based shipping rates:

Desktop

  1. Open your domestic shipping zone.
  2. In Shipping rates, click Add rate.
  3. Select Weight based as the rate type.
  4. Enter weight ranges and prices.
  5. Click Save.
Accurate product weight is required for accurate shipping charges at checkout.
