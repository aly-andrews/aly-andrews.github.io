Add products

Product listings, variants and collections.

A store cannot launch without products. Customers need complete information to buy confidently. Product quality affects SEO and conversion. Missing weight breaks shipping. Include clear titles, descriptions, pricing, media, weight and sales channel availability.

On this page

Adding products

Steps

  1. In Shopify admin, go to Products and click Add product.
  2. Enter title, description, media and pricing.
  3. Set product weight in the Shipping section.
  4. Enable Online Store under sales channels.
  5. Click Save.

Variants

Variants let you sell one product in multiple options (size, color, material) without duplicate listings. Use them when the product is fundamentally the same. Create separate products for substantially different items.

Steps

  1. Open the product in Products.
  2. In Variants, click Add options and enter names and values (e.g. size, color).
  3. Update prices, inventory and images for each variant.
  4. Click Save.

Collections

Collections group products for browsing. Manual collections let you choose each product. Automatic collections add products by rules (tag, price, type). Add collections to store navigation so customers can find them.

Steps: Manual collection

  1. Go to Products > Collections, click Create collection.
  2. Enter title and description, select Manual.
  3. Click Save, then Add products and choose items.

Automatic collections

Steps

  1. Go to Products > Collections, click Create collection.
  2. Select Automated and add conditions (tag, price, product type, vendor).
  3. Click Save.

Products that match your rules are added automatically. Use consistent tags so collections update reliably.
