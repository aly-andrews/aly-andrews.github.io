Add and organize your products

Create product listings, add variants and organize items into collections.

Your store cannot launch without complete product information. Start with basic products, then add variants and collections so customers can find what they need quickly.

Adding products

Adding a basic product

Follow these steps to add a basic product:

Desktop

  1. In your Shopify admin, go to Products.
  2. Click Add product.
  3. Enter a title, description, media and pricing details.
  4. Set product weight in the Shipping section.
  5. Enable Online Store under sales channels.
  6. Click Save.

Adding product variants

Creating variants on a product

Follow these steps to add variants to a product:

Desktop

  1. In your Shopify admin, go to Products.
  2. Open the product you want to edit.
  3. In the Variants section, click Add options.
  4. Enter option names and values such as size or color.
  5. Update prices, inventory and images for each variant.
  6. Click Save.

Creating collections

Creating a manual collection

Follow these steps to create a manual collection:

Desktop

  1. In your Shopify admin, go to Products > Collections.
  2. Click Create collection.
  3. Enter the collection title and description.
  4. Select Manual as collection type.
  5. Click Save.
  6. Click Add products and choose items.
